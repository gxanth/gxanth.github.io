ABOUT

Hi, I am Georgios. I have always been passionate about technology and understanding how things work, which led me to study Applied Mathematics with a focus on scientific computation. There, I learned how mathematical modeling and statistics can be used to describe complex systems and quantify uncertainty. Following my internship at FORTH, I realized I wanted to work in the field of data science and machine learning, and I am currently in the last year of my master's degree in Business Analytics at Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam.

During my studies, I developed a strong interest in data mining and deep learning, especially in combining both to extract valuable insights from big data. I am now looking for a thesis internship in data science and machine learning where I can work on real-world problems and apply my knowledge in practice, making a positive impact.

I have a solid background in mathematics and programming, with experience in Python and libraries such as Pandas, Polars, NumPy, Scikit-learn and PyTorch.

Besides my technical skills, I have worked as a teaching assistant for both undergraduate and graduate courses in mathematics and data science, always aiming to make complex concepts accessible and intuitive for students.
